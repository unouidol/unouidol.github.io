TwitchMiniChat Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-20

TwitchMiniChat ("TMC") is an Android Twitch chat client. This Privacy Policy explains what data TMC processes, why it is processed, how long it may be kept, and how users can request deletion or contact the developer.

1. Developer and contact

Developer: Unouidol Interactive
Privacy contact: unouidol.interactive@gmail.com

2. What data TMC may process

  • Twitch account information needed to sign in and use the app, such as Twitch username and related profile identifiers.
  • Authentication and session data needed to connect to Twitch chat.
  • Selected Twitch channels and account preferences stored by the app.
  • Push notification registration data, such as FCM token and app/device registration identifiers.
  • Public Twitch chat content shown inside the app and cached chat history needed for app functionality.
  • Message report data submitted by users, which may include message text, username, channel, message ID, and timestamp.
  • Blocked users lists and other local app settings stored on the device.
  • Operational, security, and abuse-prevention records needed to run the service and process deletion or moderation-related requests.

3. Why TMC uses data

  • To authenticate users and connect them to Twitch chat.
  • To display chat history, account settings, and selected channels.
  • To provide optional push alerts and manage device registrations.
  • To support in-app safety tools such as report and block features.
  • To protect service integrity, reduce abuse, investigate misuse, and diagnose technical issues.
  • To respond to data control and deletion requests.

4. Who data may be shared with

  • Twitch, to provide Twitch authentication and chat-related functionality.
  • Google / Firebase services used for push notifications.
  • Hosting or infrastructure providers used to operate the app backend.
  • Authorities or legal recipients where required by law or legal process.

5. Retention

Data is kept only as long as reasonably needed for app functionality, security, abuse prevention, moderation review, and deletion handling.

  • Some local settings remain on-device until the user clears app data, removes them, or uninstalls the app.
  • Some server-side records may be retained for a limited time for operational integrity, audit, abuse prevention, fraud prevention, or legal compliance.
  • Cached chat history may be retained only for a limited period needed for service functionality.

6. User controls

  • Users can report messages in-app.
  • Users can block users in-app.
  • Users can manage privacy and data controls from the app’s Safety & Privacy section.
  • Users can request deletion of app-related data. See Data Deletion.

7. Security

TMC uses reasonable technical measures to protect data in transit and at rest where applicable. No method of transmission or storage is perfectly secure.

8. Children

TMC is not directed to children. Users are responsible for complying with Twitch rules, platform requirements, and applicable law.

9. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Updated versions will replace the previous version inside the app and on the official web policy page.

10. Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests, contact: unouidol.interactive@gmail.com
